Welcome! Currently, I am resided at Denmark Technical University, in Copenhagen.

Join me as I embark on my wonderful European journey.

Copenhagen:

Such a beautiful historic and lively city. The fashion is impeccable and the natives maintain an effortless scandanavian "soft" look. Tons of food options, pretty much everything you crave you can find, price is more of the issue. Public transportation is easy to navigate and somewhat cheap.
So far, my favorite sub-city/neighborhood is Norrebro. It maintains a great multicultural population and there were tons of cute small shops, one of my favorites being a jewelry maker that averages about 35 Krone (around 6 usd). I loved people watching here as well. If you are looking for second-hand high end brands, Fresricksburg is a great place for that. Ganni is a Denmark original luxury brand that is popular and quality. Althoough buying it second hand is still pricey, its definitely worth looking for! Next, I am excited to visit Christiana, a completely lawless sub-city. Stay tuned, my one or two readers!
