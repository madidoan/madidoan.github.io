Welcome to Madison Avenue, a personal scrapbook where analog charm meets digital expression. My website is made from scratch, meaning it is imperfect, yet intentional ike a hand written letter or artisan baked good.

This site serves as an intersection and vessel for my passions of technology and creativity. As we are in the midst of a technological revolution, with an uprising comes a combative idealogy, which often is indicative of where the pendulum is going to swing next. The influx in technology invites an sub-cultural emphasis on romanticism, arts, and humanities. Here, I am leveraging our technological state to discuss topics of arts, culture, and affinity for my home, Tulsa.

Begin your journey here and explore these avenues best with curiousity and an open mind.
