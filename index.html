Welcome to Madison Avenue, an online journal where analog charm meets digital expression. My digital scrapbook is homemade like a handwritten letter or artisan baked good; Imperfect, yet intentional.


This site serves as an intersection and vessel for my passions of technology and creativity. As we find ourselves amidst a technological revolution, every uprising yearns for a combative ideology, which is often a precursor to the next swing of the cultural pendulum. The flux in technology invites a subcultural emphasis on romanticism, arts, and humanities. Here, I am leveraging our digital state to discuss topics of arts, culture, and deep affinity for my home: Tulsa.


Thank you for taking the time to not only read my rambling, but simply for clicking around on this side project that I ended up getting too invested in. I appreciate you all and thank you for engaging with me, from my ones and zeros to yours.


Begin your journey here and explore these avenues best with curiosity and an open mind.
