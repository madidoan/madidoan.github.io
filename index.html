Welcome to Madison Avenue, a online journal where analog charm meets digital expression. My website is made from scratch. Imperfect, yet intentional ike a hand written letter or artisan baked good.

This site serves as an intersection and vessel for my passions of technology and creativity. As we are amidst of a technological revolution, with every uprising comes a combative idealogy, which is often the precursor to where the pendulum is going to swing next. The flux in technology invites a sub-cultural emphasis of romanticism, arts, and humanities. Here, I am leveraging our technological state to discuss topics of arts, culture, and affinity for my home, Tulsa.

Begin your journey here and explore these avenues best with curiousity and an open mind.
